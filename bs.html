Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the on-road price of ECORIDE?

    While booking a ECORIDE electric scooter on website, you will get the indicative breakup of total on-road price. The on-road price on the date and time of final vehicle sale invoice is treated as final and binding

  • What measures have been taken to keep ECORIDE Safe from thermal incidents?

    While designing the ECORIDE, safety of the rider and vehicle is of paramount consideration. ECORIDE uses high quality advanced chemistry Lithium Ion cells sourced directly from only reputed top-quality manufacturers in the world. Within the battery itself, we use multiple internal safety measures like a Current Interruption Device that isolates the cell in any event of specific malperformance.

  • How long does the battery last on ECORIDE electric scooter?

    The ECORIDE lithium-ion battery is designed to last for 70000 kms which normally takes 7 years, provided the user ensures the lithium-ion battery is kept charged above 15% of capacity and used regularly. We offer a warranty of 3 years or 50000 kms.

  • How to book a ECORIDE online?

    It's very simple! Step 1: Create your account & complete the profile by providing basic details and OTP verifying the same. Step 2: Specify the variant and colour of ECORIDE, and your ECORIDE dealership. Step 3: Pay the booking amount to your selected ECORIDE Dealership online.

  • How far can ECORIDE electric two-wheeler go on a full charge?

    The ECORIDE electric scooter has a class-leading range of 90 km (under IDC as per AIS 040) in Eco Mode on a full charge with a new battery. The range obtained by a user shall depend on factors such as: age of the battery, road and traffic conditions, tyre condition and inflation, riding style, load on the vehicle, use of electrical accessories such as lamps and horn and mobile phone charging etc..
